Welcome to Dogccessory

— where dogs are our #1 priority —

Our story

These days it's just too overly complicated to find decent, nice looking accessories for your dog. That's why we have established a whole new clothing/accessory shop, completely dedicated for our little furry friends. We have a passionate group of over 20 people and about 8 dogs working just for you!

Why us?

Organic and responsibly produced

All of our products are being produced in carefully selected locations around the globe. Most of them are located as nearby as possible, to help reduce our carbon footprint. We don't support animal testing or any other suspicious ways of testing our goods!

There are more than 200 million stray dogs worldwide, according to the World Health Organization. The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals estimates about 3.3 million dogs enter U.S. animal shelters every years. This is why we have decided that over 20% of all our profits are given to dog shelters in need. We must protect those who cannot protect themselves.

You can help those in need by either purchasing from Dogccessories or by contacting The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals directly.

Be sure to take care of your own furry friend! You're both truly blessed to be able to spend this lifetime with each other and you should embrace it to the fullest you can. In your dog's eyes, you are their entire world. They have the most unconditional love for you that you could ever dream of! Love them the way they love you.
